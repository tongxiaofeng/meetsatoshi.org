- Completed 18 SANS Institute programs; among the first GIAC certified professionals
- GIAC profile: https://www.giac.org/certified-professional/craig-wright/107335
- GSE-Malware certification awarded October 3, 2008 (1 of 4 worldwide)
- SANS Mentor and Stay Sharp Instructor from January 2007

**SANS Reading Room publications**:
- "Requirements for Record Keeping and Document Destruction in a Digital World" (Oct 2007, 84 pages)
- "A Quantitative Time Series Analysis of Malware and Vulnerability Trends" (Oct 2006)

## Key Timeline

- **1996 Sep-Nov**: ~15 posts on Cypherpunks mailing list (cypherpunks@toad.com)
- **2003+**: Allegedly met Dave Kleiman in cryptography forums
- **2006-2008**: SANS Reading Room publications; GSE-Malware certification
- **2008-2015**: Early blog (gse-compliance.blogspot.com), Quora activity
- **2015 Dec**: Gizmodo and Wired identify Wright as potential Satoshi candidate
- **2016 May**: Public "proof" attempt fails
- **2017-2018**: Medium accounts (@ProfFaustus, @adam_selene, @craig_10243)
- **2018**: Kleiman v Wright filed; BSV fork created
- **2019+**: craigwright.net blog active; regular CoinGeek conference appearances
- **2021 Dec**: Kleiman v Wright verdict -- $100M damages to W&K Info Defense Research
- **2024 Mar**: COPA v Wright -- Justice Mellor rules Wright is not Satoshi
- **2024 Jul**: Court-ordered notice placed on craigwright.net
- **2024 Sep**: Deactivates @Dr_CSWright, moves to @CsTominaga
- **2024 Dec**: 12-month suspended sentence for contempt of court
