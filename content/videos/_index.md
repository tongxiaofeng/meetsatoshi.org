Craig Steven Wright does not maintain a personal YouTube channel. His video content appears across conference recordings, interview series, and third-party channels.

---

## Video Series

### Theory of Bitcoin (with Ryan X. Charles)

Multi-episode series produced by CoinGeek (2020). Wright discusses Bitcoin's design philosophy, technical architecture, and his vision for Bitcoin SV with Ryan X. Charles.

### Hashing it Out (with Becky Liggero)

Multi-episode CoinGeek series hosted by Becky Liggero, covering a range of Bitcoin and blockchain topics.

### CoinGeek TV & CoinGeek Backstage

Regular appearances and interview segments at CoinGeek conferences and events. CoinGeek, owned by Calvin Ayre, has been the primary media platform for Wright's public appearances.

## Conference Recordings

Wright has been a regular conference speaker, primarily at Bitcoin SV and nChain-affiliated events. See [Conferences](/conferences/) for the full list of appearances.

Most conference talks are available on the **CoinGeek YouTube channel** and affiliated platforms.

## Documentaries

### Calvin Ayre's Documentary

Calvin Ayre, billionaire backer of Bitcoin SV and owner of CoinGeek, commissioned a **documentary film** about Craig Wright's life, portraying him as Bitcoin's creator. This is separate from mainstream Bitcoin documentaries, which generally treat Wright's claims with skepticism.
