Craig Steven Wright has been a regular speaker at blockchain and technology conferences, primarily those affiliated with the Bitcoin SV ecosystem.

---

## Regular Appearances

### CoinGeek Conferences (2018--2023)

Wright has been a regular keynote speaker at CoinGeek conferences held in multiple cities worldwide:

- London
- New York
- Seoul
- Toronto
- And others

CoinGeek conferences are organized by Calvin Ayre's CoinGeek media outlet and serve as the primary conference platform for the Bitcoin SV ecosystem.

### London Blockchain Conference (2023)

Keynote presentations at the 2023 London Blockchain Conference.
