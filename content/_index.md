Welcome to **Meet Satoshi** -- a project dedicated to collecting, archiving, and organizing all publicly available content from Craig Steven Wright (CSW).

This site serves as a comprehensive, searchable resource for researchers, journalists, and anyone interested in exploring CSW's public record across blogs, social media, videos, interviews, conferences, academic papers, and more.

## Browse by Category

- [Blog Posts](/blog/) -- Articles and blog entries
- [Tweets](/tweets/) -- Twitter/X posts
- [Videos](/videos/) -- YouTube videos, recorded talks
- [Interviews](/interviews/) -- Text, audio, and video interviews
- [Conferences](/conferences/) -- Conference presentations and talks
- [Papers](/papers/) -- Academic papers and publications
