## What is Meet Satoshi?

Meet Satoshi is a comprehensive public archive dedicated to collecting and organizing all publicly available content from Craig Steven Wright (CSW).

## Why?

There is a vast amount of content spread across many platforms -- blog posts, tweets, videos, interviews, conference talks, academic papers, court documents, and more. This project aims to bring it all together in one searchable, well-organized location.

## Scope

We collect content from the following sources:

- **Blogs**: craigwright.net and other platforms where CSW has published
- **Social Media**: Twitter/X, LinkedIn
- **Video Platforms**: YouTube, conference recordings
- **Interviews**: Media appearances across text, audio, and video
- **Academic**: Papers, research publications
- **Legal**: Publicly available court documents and testimonies
- **Forums & Communities**: Slack, forum posts, and community discussions

## Contributing

This is an open-source project. If you have content to contribute or corrections to suggest, please visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/pocketsatoshi/meetsatoshi.org).

## Disclaimer

This site is a public archive. All content is sourced from publicly available materials. This project is not affiliated with Craig Steven Wright.
